Why Use Me?

You Could Spend Months Learning Alone… or Learn from Someone Who’s Done It

I’m not just a tutor—I’m a developer who’s built real-world projects and understands what employers and clients actually need. My teaching combines:

  • Hands-on Experience: Build alongside me, and learn by doing.
  • Tailored Feedback: You’ll get personalized advice that works with you, where you're at.
  • Simplified Concepts: Complex topics like APIs or extensive libraries and frameworks are broken down into an easy-to-follow model I call "the smallest layer of complexity".

What I offer

Project Assistance

I help with whatever you're currently working on. Often a school project that's due, or a work ticket in a new technology. Part pair-programming, part trouble shooting. The goal at the end is to have a working app, and a better understanding of the technical environment it's operating in.

On-going instruction

More of a long-term arrangement for people looking for dedicated instruction, either as a supplement to school, or standalone to gain full-stack web development/ software engineering skills.

Free YouTube Tutorials

The YouTube channel is a new resource, where I'll be uploading shorts and other long-form tutorials, answering questions, and providing as much knowledge as I can.
